The program is eligible for students from grade 9 to 12. As for rules, we have set requirements that include:

  • Academic transcript Grade 9 onwards
  • UN/ IGCSE Report
  • Letter of recommendation (1 teacher)
  • Personal Statement
  • Bank statement (in the last 3 months)
  • Activity resume (on application form)

You will be called for an interview if you are shortlisted as potential scholarship winner.

The scholarship awardee will be announced through Annifest Main Event on April 9th 2022. The scholarship grant letter will be sent by email.

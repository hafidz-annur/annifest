This scholarship program is for grade 11 students who are planning to study abroad. And applicants must submit all required documents to be eligible.

Click on this LINK, but make sure all documents needed are ready to attach.
Or you can click the submit document button at the bottom of this page.

The program is eligible for students in grade 11. As for rules, we have set requirements that include:

  • Academic transcript Grade 9 onwards
  • UN/ IGCSE Report
  • Letter of recommendation (1 teacher)
  • Personal Statement
  • Bank statement (in the last 3 months)
  • Activity resume (on application form)

You will be called for an interview if you are shortlisted as potential scholarship winner.

The scholarship awardee will be announced through Annifest Main Event on April 9th 2022. The scholarship grant letter will be sent by email.
